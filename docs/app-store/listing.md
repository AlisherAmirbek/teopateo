## Description

TeoPateo is a calm quit-smoking companion built for the moment you are about to smoke.

Turn your intention to quit into a practical plan based on your routines, triggers, reasons, and preferred quitting style. When a craving hits, open one-tap Craving Rescue for a focused 10-minute intervention with a timer, a replacement activity, and a reminder of why you chose to quit.

Build your quit plan
• Choose a quit date and cold-turkey or taper approach
• Prepare specific if-then plans for your smoking triggers
• Keep personal reasons and replacement activities close
• Schedule optional reminders around your plan and risky windows

Handle cravings in the moment
• Start Craving Rescue in one tap
• Ride out the next 10 minutes with a clear action
• Log the trigger and what helped
• Save a slip without shame or an automatic reset

Learn from your patterns
• Complete quick daily check-ins
• See progress, money saved, and cigarettes avoided
• Review risk windows, trigger patterns, and recent history
• Consider suggested plan adjustments based on your own logs

Ask the AI coach
• Get practical support for cravings, planning, and reflection
• Keep using the rest of the app if you decline AI data sharing
• See clear AI labels and report a reply that feels unsafe

Your quit plan, check-ins, cravings, slips, and coach history are stored locally on your device. If you enable AI coach sharing, your message and limited quit-plan context are sent to TeoPateo's coach service and its AI provider to generate a reply.

TeoPateo provides wellness and behavioral support, not medical care, diagnosis, or treatment. For medication advice or medical questions, contact a doctor, pharmacist, or quitline counselor. In an emergency, contact local emergency services.

## App Review Notes

TeoPateo is a wellness-oriented quit-smoking companion. It does not diagnose a condition, prescribe treatment, calculate medication doses, connect to a medical device, or claim a guaranteed cessation outcome.

All app features in this build are available without a subscription or reviewer account.

Suggested review path:

1. Complete onboarding, or tap **Skip for now** to reach Today.
2. On Today, tap **Start craving rescue** to open the 10-minute craving intervention.
3. Use the **Plan**, **Check-in**, and **Insights** tabs to inspect quit planning, slip recovery, and locally calculated patterns.
4. Open **Coach**, choose a starter prompt, and accept the one-time AI data-sharing consent to test the AI reply path.

AI coach disclosure:

- Coach replies are AI-generated and visibly labeled.
- The coach is not medical care and routes urgent safety language to human or emergency support.
- The app asks for explicit consent before sending a coach message or quit-plan context off-device.
- The proxy forwards a limited context to the configured AI provider. Coach history remains stored locally in the app.
- A user may decline or revoke coach sharing and continue using all non-AI features.

The app contains frequent references to smoking and tobacco solely in a cessation context. It does not sell, promote, or facilitate tobacco or vaping products.
