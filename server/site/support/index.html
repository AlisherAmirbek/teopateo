Support

For help with TeoPateo, privacy questions, or data requests, email support@teopateo.app.

Not Emergency Care

TeoPateo is quit-smoking support, not medical care, emergency care, or a replacement for a clinician. If you feel in immediate danger or have a medical emergency, contact local emergency services now. In the United States, call 911 for immediate danger, 988 for emotional crisis support, and 1-800-QUIT-NOW for tobacco quitline support.

When You Contact Support

Include the device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened. Do not send sensitive quit-plan details, full names, contact details, or medical information unless it is necessary for your request.
